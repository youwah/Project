All visitor must follow the S.O.P below:

1. Bag service will not be available. Golfers 
shall load/unload their own golf bags on to carts.

2. No sand/seed bottles shall be provided in 
carts or elsewhere on the property.

3. Employees will clean and disinfect all golf
 and push carts before and after use.

4. Designated cart staging areas shall be created 
for the safe return of carts using physical barriers or signage.
