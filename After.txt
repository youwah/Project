All visitor must follow the S.O.P below:

1. Always maintain a distance of at least 2 meters 
 from others (one flagstick length).

2. Clubhouse access and entrance points shall be 
 limited to one entry and exit point. 

3. All cubs shall map out flow and plan for 
customer/employee access to each facility to minimize
 touch points.

4. Alcohol-based hand sanitizer will be available outside 
 of washrooms and in public areas

5. All golf carts shall be limited to one rider only,
 except where two riders live in the same household.
